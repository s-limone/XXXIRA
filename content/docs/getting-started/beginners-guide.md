**XXXIRA** is a clan on Kingdom 284 (k284) in **Total Battle** — a massive multiplayer online strategy game where you build a city, train armies, explore crypts, and fight for territory on a shared world map.

This guide covers everything a new XXXIRA member needs to get started.

## The Game in One Paragraph

You build a city, produce resources, train troops, and send them out on the World Map to gather resources, kill monsters, explore crypts, and fight other players. Your city's core is the **Capitol** — its level unlocks new buildings and features. Everything feeds into your army: the bigger and better organised your troops, the more you can accomplish. Clans amplify everything — so let's work together.

## Early Game Boost

New players receive a **starter boost that lasts until Hero level 20**:
- Free construction speedups
- March speed ×10
- Revival cost –90%

**Use this window aggressively.** Push the Capitol to level 6–7, farm World Map resources, attack monsters, and run your first Crypts before the boost expires.

## Core Mechanics

### Resources
Six primary resources power everything: **Wood, Stone, Iron** (construction), **Food** (troop upkeep), **Silver** (daily operations), and **Gold** (premium currency).

> **Critical: keep food production positive at all times.** A food deficit stops all troop training completely.

See [Resources & Equipment](/docs/guides/resources) for full details.

### Buildings — Priority Order

Build in this order and keep everything within 1–2 levels of your Capitol:

| Priority | Building | Why |
|----------|---------|-----|
| 1 | **Capitol** | Gates all other buildings and features |
| 2 | **Academy** | Research is permanent and compounds forever |
| 3 | **Temple** | Reduces troop revival cost — critical for active fighters |
| 4 | **Warehouse** | Protects resources from being looted |
| 5 | **Barracks** | Each Barracks adds a parallel training queue |
| 6 | **Mansions** | Silver production — needed for everything |
| 7 | **Resource buildings** | Build 2 of each early; consolidate later |
| 8 | **Forge** | Required for crafting gear |

Long-term goal: **10 Barracks** for maximum parallel training queues.

### Research (Academy)

Research is one of the most impactful long-term investments. Keep the queue running 24/7.

**Priority:**
1. **Troop tier unlocks** — higher tiers have substantially better stats
2. **Troop training speed** — faster army replenishment
3. **Army capacity / Leadership expansion** — more troops per march
4. **Crypts research (I, II, III)** — unlocks Rare and Epic Crypts for better gear
5. **Troop health and strength** — apply to every troop type

Research uses **Valor Points** (from monster kills, crypts, PvP) and resources. Never let the Academy sit idle.

### Army & Troops

Five types of troops: Guardsmen, Specialists, Engineer Corps (leadership troops), Monsters, and Mercenaries.

| Activity | Best Troops |
|----------|------------|
| Gathering resources | **Spearmen** (infantry Guardsmen) — highest carry capacity at 250 load units |
| Epic Monster events | **Guardsmen** (mounted/flying) with the 2:1 stacking pyramid |
| PvP | **Specialists** — base attack doubles against enemy players |
| Fort/citadel attacks | Mix with 10–15% **Engineers** |

See [Units & Troops](/docs/characters/units) and [Combat & Battles](/docs/guides/combat) for full detail.

### Heroes & Captains

Your **Hero** leads every march. **Captains** provide passive bonuses to specific marches.

- **Hero recommendation for beginners:** Thaddeus (+25% Leadership) — the most straightforward power increase
- Assign the right captain per activity: Stror for gathering, Carter for Crypts, combat captains for events
- Level one captain at a time — spreading shards evenly keeps every captain weak

See [Heroes](/docs/characters/heroes) and [Captains](/docs/characters/captains).

### Crypts

Crypts are the source of **equipment materials** for your hero and captains. Better gear = better everything.

- Assign **Carter captain** to every crypt march
- Run at least one Common Crypt per day
- Unlock Crypts I, II, III research early to access better rewards

See [Crypts](/docs/guides/crypts).

### World Map

The World Map is where the game happens:
- **Resource tiles** — gather Wood, Stone, Iron, Food
- **Monsters** — kill for Valor and equipment drops
- **Crypts** — explore for materials and gear
- **Wellspring Ruins** — source of Common Tar for Crypts
- **Citadels & Elven Citadels** — contested points for clan battles; Elven Citadels drop Dragon Keys for Rare Crypts
- **Other players** — PvP combat

### Clans

XXXIRA provides:
- Clan bonuses (army strength, build speed, etc.) — active while on clan territory
- Coordinated Epic Monster events, raids, and Trials of Olympus
- Protection via Forts and Shield of Peace
- Clan chest rewards from member activity

**Stay on clan territory** to always receive bonuses. See [Clan Mechanics](/docs/guides/clan).

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

| Mistake | Why it hurts |
|---------|-------------|
| Letting food go negative | Halts all troop training |
| Spending Gold on minor speedups | Save it for revivals and major events |
| Ignoring the World Map | Valor and equipment only come from here |
| Training only one troop type | Different events need different troops |
| Not stacking troops for Epic Monster events | Costs millions more in revival Silver |
| Levelling multiple captains at once | Every captain stays weak instead of one being strong |
| Skipping Crypts | Permanent gear disadvantage |

## What to Do Each Day

- [ ] Collect from production buildings
- [ ] Complete Daily Jobs / quests
- [ ] Attack monsters (Valor + drops)
- [ ] Run a Crypt if you have Tar
- [ ] Check active events and push milestones
- [ ] Donate to clan buildings if available
