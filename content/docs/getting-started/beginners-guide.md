**XXXIRA** is a clan on Kingdom 284 (k284) in **Total Battle** — a massive multiplayer online strategy game where you build a city, train armies, explore crypts, and fight for territory on a shared world map.

This guide covers everything a new XXXIRA member needs to get started.

## The Game in One Paragraph

You build a city, produce resources, train troops, and send them out on the World Map to gather resources, kill monsters, explore crypts, and fight other players. Your city's core is the **Capitol** — its level unlocks new buildings and features. Everything feeds into your army: the bigger and better organised your troops, the more you can accomplish. Clans amplify everything — so lets work together.

## Core Mechanics

### Resources
Six primary resources power everything: **Wood, Stone, Iron** (construction), **Food** (troop upkeep), **Silver** (daily operations), and **Gold** (premium). Run out of food and troop training stops completely. See [Resources & Equipment](/docs/guides/resources) for full details.

### Buildings
Your city contains production buildings, military buildings, and research facilities. Capitol level gates most progression. Upgrade steadily — don't leave buildings idle.

### Research (Academy)
Research is one of the most important progression systems. It requires **Valor** (earned by fighting) and resources. Keep it running continuously — even small research bonuses compound over time.

### Army & Troops
Five types of troops: Guardsmen, Specialists, Engineer Corps (these are leadership troops), Monsters, and Mercenaries. Each has strengths and weaknesses. For gathering, use **Spearmen** — they carry the most resources. For combat, match troop type to the situation.

### Heroes & Captains
Your **Hero** leads every march. **Captains** provide passive bonuses and can be assigned to specific marches. Both level up over time and dramatically increase your power. See [Heroes & Captains](/docs/characters/heroes).

### World Map
The World Map is where the game happens. Key features:
- **Resource tiles** — gather Wood, Stone, Iron, Food
- **Monsters** — kill for Valor and equipment drops
- **Crypts** — explore with Tar for shards and rare materials
- **Citadels & Wellsprings** — contested points for clan battles
- **Other players** — PvP combat

### Clans
Clans are central to Total Battle. XXXIRA provides:
- Shared clan buildings (Timber Harvesting, University, Masons)
- Clan research bonuses
- Coordinated event participation (Epic Monsters, raids, Olympus, Dark Omens, Ragnarok,...)
- Protection and coordination during events

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

| Mistake | Why it hurts |
|---------|-------------|
| Letting food go negative | Halts all troop training |
| Spending Gold on minor speedups | Save it for revivals and big moments |
| Ignoring the World Map | Valor and equipment only come from here |
| Training only one troop type | Different events need different troops |
| Not stacking troops for Epic Monster events | Costs millions more in revival silver |

## What to Do Each Day

- [ ] Collect from production buildings
- [ ] Complete Daily Jobs / quests
- [ ] Attack monsters (Valor + drops)
- [ ] Run a crypt if you have Tar
- [ ] Check active events and push milestones
- [ ] Donate to clan buildings if available
