Crypts are the primary source of **equipment crafting materials** in Total Battle. Running crypts consistently is how you gear up your hero and captains — skipping them means permanent long-term weakness.

## Three Crypt Types

| Type | Costs | Rewards | Research Required |
|------|-------|---------|-------------------|
| **Common** | Tar | Crafting materials for standard gear | Crypts I |
| **Rare** | Rare Tar | Gems for socketing into equipment | Crypts II + Dragon Key |
| **Epic** | Epic Tar | Materials for unique-tier equipment | Crypts III |

**Common and Epic Crypts** have a chance to yield **double materials** per hit. Rare Crypts do not.

## How Crypts Work

A crypt has a total HP pool. Each march is one **hit** and deals a fixed amount of damage. The crypt clears when its HP reaches zero.

Key formula: `Total Tar needed = (Crypt HP ÷ Damage per hit) × Cost per hit`

Three stats affect your efficiency:

| Stat | Effect |
|------|--------|
| **Exploration Cost Bonus** | Reduces Tar consumed per hit — most reliable improvement |
| **Exploration Efficiency** | Increases progress per hit — only worth investing in when it pushes you below a whole-hit threshold |
| **Double-Strike probability** | Chance that one hit counts as two without extra Tar — pure bonus |

> **Priority rule:** Invest in Cost reduction first. Efficiency only matters when the bonus moves you past a whole-hit threshold; otherwise it wastes shards.

Carrying **10% more Tar than the base requirement** triggers a bonus efficiency multiplier. Always carry slightly over.

## Tar Sources

| Tar Type | How to Obtain |
|----------|--------------|
| Common Tar | Wellspring Ruins on the World Map; daily chests |
| Rare Tar | Dragon Keys from Elven Citadels on the World Map |
| Epic Tar | Tournament rewards; special event milestones |

Base maximum march time is **20 minutes**. The Ayrin hero, research, and talent points extend this — longer marches mean fewer trips to start manually.

## Carter Captain

Carter is the dedicated crypt captain. Always assign him to crypt marches.

- His bonuses reduce Tar cost and increase exploration efficiency
- His bonuses are **capped at a certain level** — check in-game to confirm the current cap; do not waste shards past it
- Investing in Carter early is one of the highest-return decisions in the game

## Why Crypts Matter

Crypts drop the crafting materials for every equipment slot on your hero and captains. Better gear means:
- Higher army strength and health in every fight
- More Leadership (larger marches)
- Better crypt runs (dedicated crypt gear reduces Tar cost further)

The gear loop accelerates over time. Start running crypts as early as possible.

## Daily Habit

Run at least one Common Crypt per day. Even partial clears generate materials. The players who gear up fastest are the ones who run crypts daily without exception.
