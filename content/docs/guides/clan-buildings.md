Clan buildings sit on the World Map inside clan territory and are built and upgraded by clan Leaders and Superiors. They convert city resources into **clan resources** used to upgrade clan structures and activate bonuses.

Members donate city resources to clan buildings via **caravan** — there is no tax on donations to clan buildings (unlike the 20% tax on player-to-player resource transfers).

## How Donations Work

1. Tap or click a clan building on the World Map
2. Send resources via caravan from your city
3. Resources accumulate automatically in the Clan Capital — no manual collection needed
4. Each building has **six efficiency levels** based on how much resource is currently stored — keep buildings stocked for maximum output

---

## Clan University

Converts **Silver** → **Scientific Tractates** (Red Books)

Scientific Tractates are required for all **Clan Research** in the Capital.

| Stored Silver | Efficiency |
|--------------|------------|
| > 0 | Level 1 (minimum) |
| > 4M | Level 2 |
| > 7M | Level 3 |
| > 14M | Level 4 |
| > 30M | Level 5 |
| > 60M | Level 6 (maximum) |

- Consumption: **300,000 Silver/hour** (constant regardless of efficiency level)
- Maximum per clan: **3 University buildings**
- Construction cost: 9M Lumber + 9M Iron + 9M Stone

> Keep the University stocked above 60M Silver for maximum Tractate output.

## Clan Temple

Converts **Food** → **Religious Tractates**

Religious Tractates are required to **activate Clan Bonuses** (army strength, construction speed, etc.) from the Capital.

| Stored Food | Efficiency |
|------------|------------|
| > 0 | Level 1 |
| > 6M | Level 2 |
| > 12M | Level 3 |
| > 25M | Level 4 |
| > 50M | Level 5 |
| > 100M | Level 6 |

- Consumption: **500,000 Food/hour** (constant)
- Maximum per clan: **3 Temple buildings**
- Construction cost: 7M Lumber + 7M Iron + 7M Stone

## Clan Masons

Converts **Stone** → **Cement**

Cement is one of the three clan resources required to **upgrade clan buildings**.

| Stored Stone | Efficiency |
|-------------|------------|
| > 0 | Level 1 |
| > 3M | Level 2 |
| > 6M | Level 3 |
| > 12M | Level 4 |
| > 25M | Level 5 |
| > 50M | Level 6 |

- Consumption: **250,000 Stone/hour**
- Maximum per clan: **3 Masons buildings**
- Construction cost: 5M Lumber + 5M Iron + 5M Stone
- Best tile: Gentle slope terrain (up to 90% bonus)

## Summary Table

| Building | City Resource In | Clan Resource Out | Max per Clan | Build Cost |
|----------|-----------------|------------------|-------------|------------|
| University | Silver (300k/hr) | Scientific Tractates | 3 | 9M each |
| Temple | Food (500k/hr) | Religious Tractates | 3 | 7M each |
| Masons | Stone (250k/hr) | Cement | 3 | 5M each |
| Foundry | Iron (250k/hr) | Steel | 3 | 5M each |

All buildings: maximum 90% tile bonus available. Only Clan Leaders and Superiors can construct buildings on clan territory.
