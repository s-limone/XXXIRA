Key terms used in Total Battle and on this wiki.

---

## A–C

**Academy** — Building that researches technologies providing permanent bonuses to combat, production, construction, and more. Keep it running 24/7.

**Authority** — The capacity limit that controls how many Mercenaries you can include in a march.

**Barracks** — Building that trains troops. Each additional Barracks adds a parallel training queue.

**Capitol** — Your city's core building. Its level gates most other buildings and features.

**Carter** — The Crypt exploration captain. Essential for all crypt runs; bonus cap at level 300.

**Cement** — Clan resource produced by Clan Masons (from Stone). Required for clan building upgrades.

**Clan Bonus** — Stat bonus activated via Religious Tractates in the Clan Capital. Requires your city to be on clan territory.

**Conflagration** — Status a Fort enters after being fully destroyed. Lasts 40 hours; Fort cannot be rebuilt during this period.

**Conquest Points (CP)** — Earned from PvP, Shadow Invasion, and CP items. Used for Academy research and unlocking Dustan.

**Crypt** — Dungeon explored using Tar. Yields crafting materials in Common, Rare, and Epic tiers.

---

## D–G

**Dominance** — The capacity limit that controls how many Monster-type troops you can include in a march.

**Dragon** — A special unit that cannot die in battle. Adds Dominance-slot power. Levelled via Dragon Treats and Dragon Orbs.

**Dragon Keys** — Required for Rare Crypts. Obtained by defeating Elven Citadels on the World Map.

**Dustan** — The Conquest Points captain. Passive bonus; keep in city. Bonus cap at level 300.

**Engineer Corps** — Leadership troops with bonus attack vs. fortifications (citadels, walls). Troop tiers C1–C9.

**Epic Monster** — A powerful world-map boss that requires stacked troops and often captain/hero participation. Source of major event rewards.

**Epic Tar** — Required for Epic Crypts. Earned from tournament rewards and event milestones.

**Forge** — Building used to craft equipment for heroes and captains.

**Fort** — Clan defensive structure. Protects the Clan Capital. Max 4 per clan.

**Food** — Resource produced by Farms. Required for troop upkeep — running out halts all troop training.

**Guardsmen** — Leadership troops with bonus attack vs. monsters. Troop tiers G1–G9.

---

## H–M

**Hero** — Your primary commander who leads every march. Their level determines Leadership, Dominance, and Authority limits.

**Heroic Monster** — World-map boss requiring Hero + Captains + clan reinforcements. Near-impossible solo.

**Leadership** — The capacity limit controlling how many trained troops (Guardsmen, Specialists, Engineers) you can send in a march.

**Mercenaries** — Troops governed by your Authority limit. Attack last in combat; survive longest when stacked correctly.

**Monsters (troop type)** — Troops governed by your Dominance limit. Highest base attack and health. Four sub-types: Beast, Elemental, Giant, Dragon. Tiers M3–M9.

---

## P–R

**Pocket Fort** — A spare Fort held in reserve for optimised defence rotation during attacks.

**Portal** — A march portal on the World Map. If left open, enemies can attack through it bypassing city walls. Close when not in use.

**Rare Tar** — Required for Rare Crypts. Obtained from Dragon Keys (dropped by Elven Citadels).

**Religious Tractates** — Clan resource produced by Clan Temples (from Food). Required to activate Clan Bonuses.

**Revival** — Bringing defeated troops back. Cost scales with troop tier and your Temple level. Higher Temple = cheaper revival.

---

## S–Z

**Scientific Tractates (Red Books)** — Clan resource produced by Clan Universities (from Silver). Required for Clan Research.

**Shield of Peace** — Clan defensive shield that activates for 20 hours after all Forts enter Conflagration — but only if pre-charged. Must be charged proactively.

**Silver** — Resource produced by Mansions. Used for most transactions: troop training, revival, daily costs.

**Specialists** — Leadership troops with base attack doubled against other players. Tiers S1–S9.

**Stacking** — Combat technique where troops are arranged so cheap troops absorb damage first, allowing your strongest troops to attack more times. The most important advanced mechanic in the game.

**Stror** — The gathering captain. Only captain with resource gathering bonuses. Always use for gathering marches.

**Tar** — Consumable resource used for Common Crypt exploration. Collected from Wellspring Ruins on the World Map.

**Temple** — Building that reduces troop revival cost. High priority for active combat players.

**Valor Points (VP)** — Earned from defeating monsters, crypts, and PvP. Used to fund Academy research.

**Warehouse** — Building that protects resources from being looted. Keep it levelled.

**Wellspring Ruins** — World Map tiles that produce Tar for Common Crypt exploration.
