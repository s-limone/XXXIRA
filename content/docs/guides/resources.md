## Primary Resources

| Resource | Produced By | Key Use |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| **Wood** | Lumber Mills | Buildings and basic construction |
| **Stone** | Quarries | Buildings and research |
| **Iron** | Mines | Troops and equipment |
| **Food** | Farms | Troop upkeep — **running out stops troop training entirely** |
| **Silver** | Mansions | Most transactions, troop training, daily costs |
| **Gold** | Premium (shop/events) | Revivals, speedups, special items |

> **Critical:** Keep food production positive at all times. A food deficit blocks all troop training.

## Special Resources

| Resource | How to Obtain | Used For |
|----------|--------------|---------|
| **Tar** | Wellsprings on World Map, chests | Crypt exploration |
| **Valor** | Combat: monster attacks, crypts, PvP | Research advancement (Academy) |
| **Wellspring materials** | Wellspring tiles | Special crafting and upgrades |

## Resource Farming

### In-City Production

Upgrade your production buildings (Farm, Lumber Mill, Quarry, Mine, Mansions) regularly — they are a consistent passive income source. Capitol level unlocks additional building slots.

### World Map Gathering

Send troops to resource tiles on the World Map. Key tips:
- **Higher level tiles hold more resources** — target the highest level your march can clear
- Use **Spearmen** (Guardsmen/infantry type, 250 load) for gathering — Riders carry 200 and Archers only 100
- Equip a gathering-focused hero loadout to increase march load and speed
- Warehouses protect a portion of resources if you are attacked while gathering

### Clan Resources

Joining a clan unlocks additional resource rewards:
- Clan buildings provide bonuses (Timber Harvesting, University, Masons)
- Clan research improves gathering and production rates
- Clan members can donate resources to each other

### Daily Quests & Events

Complete daily quests and participate in events for reliable resource top-ups. These often reward Silver, Gold, and special materials.

## Resource Protection

- **Warehouses** protect a fixed amount of resources from attackers
- **Peace Shields** grant temporary immunity from attacks — use them when logging off with large stockpiles

## Equipment

Equipment is crafted in the **Forge** and provides stat bonuses to your hero and troops. Equip different sets depending on your activity:

| Set Type | Best For |
|----------|----------|
| **Combat / PvP set** | Attacking players, PvP events |
| **Monster hunting set** | Crypt runs, Epic Monster events |
| **Gathering set** | World Map resource farming |

Craft materials come from crypt exploration, monster kills, and events. Prioritise completing a full set before upgrading individual pieces — full set bonuses are significant.

## Gems & Enchantments

Gems slot into equipment to add extra stats. Enchantments apply further multipliers. Both systems scale with your overall power — invest in them after you have a solid equipment base.

## Artifacts

Artifacts are powerful items that provide large bonuses. Types include combat artifacts, gathering artifacts, and production artifacts. Obtain them through:
- Seasonal events
- High-level crypt exploration
- Event milestone rewards

Prioritise artifacts that match your current focus (e.g. monster-hunting artifacts during Epic Monster events).
