The **Hero** is your primary commander. 

## Hero Mechanics

### Leveling
Heroes gain experience through combat, quests, and items. Higher level = larger march limits and more skill points.

Skill points can be allocated across several trees. Focus on one path rather than spreading points thin:

| Tree | Best For |
|------|----------|
| Combat / PvP | Player vs Player, attack/defence bonuses |
| Monster Hunting | Crypt runs, Epic Monster events |
| Gathering | World Map resource farming |
| City / Development | Building speed, production bonuses |

> Respeccing skills usually costs Gold or a reset item — plan your build before spending points.

### Leadership Limit
Your Leadership limit (max troops in a march) scales with Hero level plus bonuses from equipment, research, titles, and captains. This is the key number to grow for more powerful marches.

## Equipment for Heroes

Your hero wears equipment crafted in the Forge. Swap sets depending on your activity:
- **Combat set** — for PvP and raids
- **Gathering set** — for World Map farming
- **Monster hunting set** — for crypts and Epic Monster events

See [Resources & Equipment](/docs/guides/resources) for crafting details.
