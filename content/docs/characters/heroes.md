The **Hero** is your primary commander — every march is led by them and their level directly determines your Leadership limit (how many troops you can send).

## Hero Selection

There are 15 heroes. Each has a unique passive ability. You can only use one at a time, so choose based on your current playstyle.

| Hero | Key Bonus | Best For |
|------|-----------|---------|
| **Thaddeus** | +25% Leadership to hero and captains | Bigger marches — most impactful for beginners |
| **Haemon** | Clan bonuses ×2 | Clan-first players on clan territory |
| **Ryjong** | Army and captain march speed ×2 | Fast farming and rapid strikes |
| **Svyatogor** | +troop strength and health in solo marches | Independent combat and monster hunting |
| **Matemhain** | +200% Engineer strength/health; wall rebuild in 30 s | Defensive or engineering-focused city |
| **Ayrin** | Unlimited march duration; cross-kingdom movement | Inter-kingdom events (Clash of Kingdoms) |
| **Alrick** | Enemy marches on your city take ≥4 hours | Passive defence; hard to siege |
| **Faron** | +900% delegates from titles | Clash for the Throne political play |
| **Yamato** | Can eliminate enemy hero after scouting | Offensive operations |
| **Meriones** | Hero stats ×1.5 during combat | Combat amplification |
| **Garvel / Julia** | No special bonus | Not recommended for veteran play |

> **Starting recommendation:** Thaddeus gives +25% Leadership across the board — the most straightforward power boost for any new account. Switch to Haemon once you're consistently on clan territory.

## Talent Points

Talent points are earned every hero level. From **level 86 onward you receive 10 points per level**. There is no level cap stated — plan for the long term.

Four talent branches:

| Branch | What it does |
|--------|-------------|
| **Battle Tactics** | Chance to deal double damage in combat |
| **Expert Battle Tactics** | Troop training speed + army carrying capacity |
| **Economy** | Bonus effects on resource tiles you're farming |
| **Archaeology** | Bonus XP in Crypts |

**Priority for most players:**
1. Max **Battle Tactics** first — double-damage chance applies to every fight
2. Then **Expert Battle Tactics** — faster training and bigger marches
3. **Archaeology** if you run Crypts frequently (pays off long-term)
4. **Economy** only for dedicated gathering alts

> Talents apply **at all times regardless of where your hero is**. Respeccing costs 18,000 Gold (or a reset item from events). Plan before you spend.

## Leadership Limit

Your Leadership limit determines how many trained troops you can send in one march. It scales with:
- Hero level
- Captain bonuses
- Equipment (dedicated leadership gear)
- Research (Academy)
- Titles (held via Clash for the Throne)
- Dragon presence in the march

Growing your Leadership limit is the single most impactful progression you can make — a bigger march means more damage, more resources gathered, and more event points per attack.

## Equipment Sets

Your hero has **6 equipment slots**. Craft multiple sets and swap before each activity:

| Set | Prioritise |
|-----|-----------|
| **Combat** | Army strength, army health, leadership |
| **Gathering** | Carrying capacity, gathering speed |
| **Monster hunting** | Crypt XP, army strength vs monsters |
