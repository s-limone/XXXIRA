Total Battle has five categories of troops, each with different strengths, costs, and roles in combat and events.

## Troop Categories

| Category | March Limit | Attack Order | Special Bonus |
|----------|------------|-------------|---------------|
| **Guardsmen** | Leadership | 1st | Bonus attack vs. **monsters** |
| **Specialists** | Leadership | 2nd | Bonus attack vs. **other players** (base attack ×2 in PvP) |
| **Engineer Corps** | Leadership | 3rd | Bonus attack vs. **fortifications** |
| **Monsters** | Dominance | After leadership | Higher base attack and health; four sub-types |
| **Mercenaries** | Authority | Last | Bonuses vs. players or monsters depending on type |

## Troop Tiers

Troops are trained in tiers. Tier notation by category:
- Guardsmen: **G1–G9**
- Specialists: **S1–S9**
- Engineer Corps: **C1–C9**
- Monsters: **M3–M9**

Higher tiers are significantly stronger and cost proportionally more to train and revive. Always train the highest tier available — lower tiers are primarily useful as **sacrifice layers** in stacking builds.

## Monster Sub-types

Monsters come in four sub-types, each with different traits:
- **Beast**
- **Elemental**
- **Giant**
- **Dragon**

## Combat Trait System

Each unit has three traits that determine its vulnerabilities and offensive advantages against specific unit classifications: **Melee, Mounted, Ranged, Flying**. Check each unit's traits to understand matchups — it is more nuanced than a simple rock-paper-scissors system.

## Gathering Troops

For resource gathering on the World Map, **load capacity** matters more than combat power.

The gathering-relevant unit names and their categories:

| Unit Name | Category | Load Capacity |
|-----------|----------|--------------|
| Spearmen | Guardsmen (Infantry) | 250 per unit |
| Riders | Engineer Corps (Mounted) | 200 per unit |
| Archers | Specialists (Ranged) | 100 per unit |

Use **Spearmen** (infantry/Guardsmen type) for gathering marches whenever possible.

## Troop Revival

All troops that die can be revived in the **Hospital** using Silver and Gold. Key factors affecting revival cost:
- **Temple level** — higher Temple level reduces revival cost significantly
- **Novice Boost** — ~10× cheaper revival during early-game boost period
- **Captain bonuses** — some captains reduce revival costs
- **Event buffs** — occasional event-wide revival discounts

## Training Priority

1. Train your highest available tier first
2. Keep a reserve of lower tiers for **stacking sacrifice layers** (see [Combat & Battles](/docs/guides/combat))
3. Keep Mercenaries stocked with **Epic Monster Hunters** for event participation
