Captains can lead marches alone or alongside your hero. Each provides unique bonuses. You can have multiple captains active simultaneously, each leading their own march or providing passive city bonuses.

**Cap at level 300:** Carter, Doria, and Dustan all stop giving bonus gains at level 300 — do not waste shards past that point.

---

## Starter Captains

Unlocked automatically after the tutorial.

### Aydae
- **Bonus:** Guardsmen Strength & Health
- **Best for:** Defending your city, early monster hunting, Guardsmen-heavy marches
- **Notes:** Solid early-game choice while building toward premium combat captains

### Logos
- **Bonus:** Specialists Strength & Health
- **Best for:** Specialist-heavy armies, Silver farming — *always use Logos when Silver farming*

### Tengel
- **Bonus:** March Speed (+Level ÷ 2)%, March Range (+Level ÷ 5)%
- **At level 500:** +250% march speed, +100% range
- **Best for:** Long-distance gathering, rapid reinforcements, hit-and-run attacks
- **Avoid:** Combat — Tengel provides zero combat bonus

## Academy Captains

Unlocked through Academy research or Summoning Circle.

### Carter — Crypts *(Essential)*
- **Bonus:** Crypt exploration efficiency + Tar capacity
- **Level cap for bonus: 300** — stop levelling at 300
- **Best for:** Every crypt run — no other captain offers crypt bonuses
- **Gear:** Equip Crypt-specific gear on Carter

### Stror — Gathering
- **Bonus:** Resource gathering speed + army carrying capacity
- **Best for:** All gathering marches — pair with Spearmen for maximum haul
- **Critical:** Bonuses only apply when Stror *leads* the march

### Dustan — Conquest Points *(Essential)*
- **Bonus:** Conquest Points bonus (passive)
- **Level cap for bonus: 300**
- **Best for:** All CP activities — Shadow City, PvP, CP runs
- **How to unlock:** Academy research using Conquest Points
- **Notes:** Expensive to unlock, but dramatically increases all future CP earnings. Essential for PvP players and Clash for the Throne.

### Doria — Valor Points
- **Bonus:** Valor Points bonus (passive city bonus — must remain in city, not marching)
- **Level cap for bonus: 300**
- **Best for:** All activities that generate Valor Points
- **Tip:** Pair with Dustan for combined VP + CP passive bonuses

### Ingrid — Monster Troops
- **Bonus:** Strength & Health of Monster-type troops
- **Best for:** Monster-heavy stacking marches in Epic Monster events
- **Notes:** Only valuable if you have significant Monster troop investment; for mixed armies use Cleopatra

### Brann — Engineer Corps
- **Bonus:** Strength & Health of Engineers
- **Best for:** Engineer-heavy armies, citadel/fort sieges

### Ye Ho-Sung — Melee
- **Bonus:** Strength & Health of Melee troops
- **Best for:** Melee-heavy armies, heroic monster levels where Melee units are optimal
- **Premium alternative:** Leonidas

### Bernard — Ranged
- **Bonus:** Strength & Health of Ranged troops
- **Best for:** Ranged-heavy armies
- **Premium alternative:** Minamoto

### Lucius — Mounted
- **Bonus:** Strength & Health of Mounted troops
- **Best for:** Cavalry-focused armies
- **Premium alternative:** Alexander

### Brunhild — Flying
- **Bonus:** Strength & Health of Flying troops
- **Best for:** Flying-heavy armies
- **Premium alternative:** Wu Zetian

### Proscope — Scouting
- **Bonus:** Strength & Health of Scouts + Scout Capacity & Speed
- **Best for:** Scouting operations and intelligence gathering

### Xi Guiying — Ranged & Scouts
- **Bonus:** Strength of Flying troops (dual bonus — unique for Academy type)
- **Unlock:** Academy research + Summoning Circle
- **Best for:** Flying combat, CP Runs

## Premium Captains

Obtained via Summoning Circle, events, or special activities.

### Beowulf — Best Combat Captain *(Seasonal Kingdom)*
- **Bonus:** Strength AND Health of entire army (dual bonus)
- **Unlock:** Seasonal Kingdom event — requires reaching Guardsmen Level 6 within 3 months
- **Best for:** Everything — endgame all-purpose combat captain
- **Notes:** One of the hardest captains to obtain. If you have him, use him for all combat.

### Cleopatra — Second Best Combat
- **Bonus:** Strength of entire army
- **Best for:** All combat — PvP, Epic Monsters, mixed armies
- **Notes:** If you have Beowulf use him; Cleopatra is the next best for pure damage output

### Sofia — Army Health
- **Bonus:** Health of entire army
- **Best for:** Defensive battles, tough content — pairs well with Cleopatra
- **Premium version of:** No academy equivalent (Ramses II is the event-exclusive upgrade)

### Ramses II — Army Health (Event Exclusive)
- **Bonus:** Health of entire army (higher stats than Sofia)
- **Unlock:** Exclusively through Pharaoh's Bazaar event
- **Best for:** Defensive battles, difficult combat, heroic monster encounters

### Hercules — Epic Monsters Only *(Triumphal Challenge)*
- **Bonus:** Strength of entire army vs **Epic Monsters ONLY**
- **Unlock:** Purchasing fragments from the Triumphal Challenge shop
- **Critical:** Bonuses apply *exclusively* to Epic Monsters. Does NOT work vs regular monsters or players.
- **Best for:** Chimera, Basilisk, Briareus, Doomsday, and all Epic Monster events
- **Do not use** Hercules for PvP or regular monster hunting — use Cleopatra or Beowulf instead

### Skadi — Guardsmen *(Seasonal Kingdom)*
- **Bonus:** Health AND Strength of Guardsmen
- **Unlock:** Same Seasonal Kingdom event as Beowulf
- **Notes:** One of the rarest captains in the game — obtained alongside Beowulf
- **Best for:** Guardsmen-heavy marches, Epic Monster events

### Heimdall — Army Strength *(Seasonal Kingdom 5)*
- **Bonus:** Army Strength across all troop types
- **Unlock:** Seasonal Kingdom 5 (requires multiple seasons, fragment collection)
- **Best for:** Epic Monsters, PvP, Seasonal Kingdom, raids
- **Notes:** Long-term investment; maximum potential at 7 stars

### Amanitore — Group Activities
- **Bonus:** Strength AND Health of army **in group activities only**
- **Applies to:** Group marches, raids, reinforcements
- **Does NOT apply:** Solo marches
- **Best for:** Raids, CP runs (requires 1+ other player), heroic monsters, group events
- **Tip:** Pair with Dustan for maximum raid efficiency. Essential for clans doing regular raids.

### Leonidas — Melee (Premium)
- **Bonus:** Strength & Health of Melee troops
- **Best for:** Melee-heavy armies, heroic monster levels where Melee dominates
- **Academy alternative:** Ye Ho-Sung

### Alexander — Mounted (Premium)
- **Bonus:** Strength & Health of Mounted troops
- **Best for:** Mounted-heavy armies, mobile warfare
- **Academy alternative:** Lucius

### Minamoto — Ranged (Premium)
- **Bonus:** Strength & Health of Ranged troops
- **Best for:** Ranged-heavy armies
- **Academy alternative:** Bernard

### Wu Zetian — Flying (Premium)
- **Bonus:** Strength & Health of Flying troops
- **Best for:** Flying-heavy armies — significantly increases flying health, may require ratio adjustments in stacking
- **Academy alternative:** Brunhild

### Helen — Food & Training Speed *(Economy)*
- **Bonus:** Food Production Speed + Troop Training Speed
- **Type:** Passive city bonus — must be **active in city**, not deployed on march
- **Best for:** Mass troop training, preventing food deficits, post-battle rebuilding, maintaining G8/G9 armies
- **Unlock:** Union Triumphal Event (primary), Summoning Circle, limited events
- **Tip:** Activate Helen + equip Honored Ruler armor → begin troop training → swap after training starts. Part of the economy trio: Helen (training) + Aurora (construction) + Farhad (research)

### Aurora — Construction Speed *(Economy)*
- **Bonus:** Construction speed
- **Type:** Passive city bonus — must be active in city
- **Best for:** City development and long construction projects
- **Notes:** Does NOT need to lead marches — just keep her in a captain slot in your city

### Farhad — Research Speed *(Economy)*
- **Bonus:** Research speed
- **Type:** Passive city bonus — must be active in city
- **Best for:** All Academy research — significant time savings on long projects
- **Strategy:** Keep stationed during research phases; switch to combat captains for military operations

## Quick Reference

| Activity | Captain |
|----------|---------|
| All combat (general) | Beowulf → Cleopatra → Aydae |
| Epic Monster events | Hercules (epic only) + Beowulf/Cleopatra/Skadi |
| PvP | Beowulf → Cleopatra |
| Gathering | **Stror** (no substitute) |
| Crypts | **Carter** (no substitute) |
| Valor Points | Doria (passive, stay in city) |
| Conquest Points | Dustan (passive, stay in city) |
| Raids & group marches | Amanitore + Dustan |
| Troop training speed | Helen (stay in city) |
| Construction speed | Aurora (stay in city) |
| Research speed | Farhad (stay in city) |
