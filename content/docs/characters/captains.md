Captains are special characters that provide powerful bonuses when assigned to a march or your city.

## How Captains Work

- Assign a captain to a march to apply their bonus to that specific action
- Some captains excel in combat, others in gathering or monster events
- Captains level up through **shards** (obtained from events, crypts, shop packs)
- Prioritise levelling one captain at a time rather than spreading shards evenly
