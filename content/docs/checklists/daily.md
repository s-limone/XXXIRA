Do these every day. Small consistent actions compound into massive long-term power. Tick each item off as you go — your progress saves automatically.

---

## Every Day

- [ ] Collect from all production buildings (Farms, Lumber Mills, Quarries, Mines, Mansions)
- [ ] Start or continue a research item in the Academy
- [ ] Queue troop training in all Barracks
- [ ] Check food balance — if negative, fix it before training more troops

---

## World Map

- [ ] Attack monsters for Valor and equipment drops
- [ ] Send at least one gathering march (Spearmen + Stror captain)
- [ ] Check Wellspring Ruins — collect Tar for Crypts
- [ ] Move any idle marches back to city

---

## Crypts

- [ ] Run at least one Common Crypt (assign Carter captain)
- [ ] Open any pending clan chests before they expire (20-hour window)

---

## Daily Jobs & Quests

- [ ] Complete Daily Jobs (resets every 24 hours — free Silver, Gold, resources)
- [ ] Check Achievement progress and claim any completed rewards
- [ ] Check any active limited-time event milestones and push progress

---

## Clan

- [ ] Donate to clan buildings if donation is available
- [ ] Check clan chat for any coordination requests or war updates
- [ ] Reinforce any Forts if they took damage

---

## Weekly (pick a day)

- [ ] Review your hero talent allocation — are you still on the right tree?
- [ ] Review equipment — craft any new gear from accumulated materials
- [ ] Check Forge queue — is it idle?
- [ ] Check Capitol and Academy levels — is anything ready to upgrade?
- [ ] Review troop counts by tier — do you need to train higher tiers?
