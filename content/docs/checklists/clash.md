Clash for the Throne runs approximately every two weeks. Preparation before it starts matters as much as play during it.

See [Clan Mechanics](/docs/guides/clan) for the full event explanation.

---

## 3–7 Days Before Clash

- [ ] Push resources below your **Warehouse protection level** — unprotected resources can be looted
- [ ] Stock up on **Silver** — PvP revival is expensive
- [ ] Check your **Shield of Peace** charge — make sure it's pre-charged
- [ ] Reinforce all **Forts** to full health
- [ ] Check that all Forts are at full HP — partial damage Forts must be rebuilt immediately
- [ ] Coordinate with clan leadership on attack targets and timing
- [ ] Switch to **Faron hero** if you're targeting a Senator/King position (Faron +900% delegate bonus)

---

## Day 1 — Earn Conquest Points (CP)

- [ ] Check if you're in the top 100 ranking at event start — top 100 become Senators
- [ ] Identify best CP sources for your playstyle:
  - [ ] Shadow Invasion participation (highest CP source)
  - [ ] Defeating enemy armies in PvP
  - [ ] Using CP reward items from inventory
- [ ] **Note:** Top-100 players cannot use Shield of Peace on Day 1 — coordinate clan protection
- [ ] Attack actively — more CP = higher Senate rank = more voting influence
- [ ] Check Forts regularly during Day 1 — repair any partial damage immediately
- [ ] Close any open march portals when not actively using them

---

## Day 2 — Voting

- [ ] Confirm your Senator status (top 100 from Day 1)
- [ ] Coordinate vote with XXXIRA clan leadership — who are we supporting?
- [ ] Cast your vote before the voting window closes

---

## After Clash

- [ ] Revive troops from PvP battles
- [ ] Collect Clash reward chests and milestone prizes
- [ ] Recharge Shield of Peace for next Clash
- [ ] Repair any damaged Forts — don't leave them in Conflagration status longer than necessary
- [ ] Re-stock Silver and resources for the next cycle
- [ ] Review CP ranking — what activities earned the most points?
